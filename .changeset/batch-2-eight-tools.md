Eight new tools from the phase-2 opportunities review, batch 2: merge-audio and audio-speed (the thinnest category, riding the existing ffmpeg engine — join clips, change speed with natural pitch), images-to-gif (animated GIF from stills via gifenc), epub-to-text (completes the document trio next to pdf-to-text/docx-to-text), har-sanitize (strip cookies/tokens/auth from network captures before sharing — redacts in place, stays DevTools-loadable), vcf-to-csv (contacts to spreadsheet, sibling of ics-to-csv), toml-convert (TOML⇄JSON via smol-toml), and ip-subnet-calc (CIDR math with correct /31 and /32 handling). All free, all local, all with curated chain suggestions that lead with free tools.
