Six new tools from the phase-2 opportunities review: heic-to-jpg (iPhone HEIC/HEIF to JPG/PNG/WebP via libheif wasm — unlocks Apple photos for every image tool), compress-image-to-size (hit an exact KB target for passport/visa/upload forms; binary-searches quality then optionally downscales; never touches already-small files), image-collage (strip or grid from 2-20 photos), pixelate-region (block-pixelate a region — or everything — before sharing screenshots), ics-to-csv (calendar exports to CSV/JSON for the spreadsheet suite), and x509-decode (PEM/DER certificate to a readable report with SANs, validity, and fingerprints). All run entirely in the browser, all wired into chain suggestions.
